82-183 AI for Global Humanities, Spring 2024

Teaching assistants —TBA
Course links — Canvas site, Course webpage

Course objective — This innovative course will explore the multifaceted realm of AI through three themes: AI for Education, AI for Artistic Creation, and AI for Sustainable Societies and the Environment. Through a combination of theoretical knowledge, hands-on assignments, and creative projects, students will have a better understanding of the transformative potential of AI, which may reshape our perception of lifelong learning, arts, and society.
